# Business Strategy

Load the `mba:strategy` skill and apply it to the user's challenge.

## Steps

1. **Load the skill.** Read `skills/strategy/SKILL.md` for core frameworks and instructions.

2. **Clarify the strategic question.** Determine whether this is about growth strategy, competitive positioning, revenue model design, organizational alignment, or pressure-testing a strategy draft.

3. **Select the right framework:**
   - **T-Algorithm** — Competitive/growth strategy analysis
   - **Connected Strategic Stack** — Planning and execution alignment, OKRs
   - **AI-Augmented Decision Making** — Strategic agents for research and debate
   - **Adversarial Debate (Rumelt Lite)** — Pressure-test strategy drafts

4. **Consult references as needed:**
   - `skills/strategy/references/t-algorithm.md` — Full T-Algorithm with examples
   - `skills/strategy/references/strategic-planning.md` — Strategic planning, OKRs, AI-augmented decisions
   - `skills/strategy/references/adversarial-debate.md` — Rumelt framework, bad strategy detection, debate protocol

5. **Apply the framework rigorously** and provide actionable recommendations.
